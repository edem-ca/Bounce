WELCOME TO BOUNCE!

Controls:
-Press the Up Key To jump
-Press the Down Key to drop with super speed and hit enemies with some extra oomph!
-Press Left and Right...to move left and right

Objectives/Rules:
-Bounce all the Trouncermen out of the club!!! This club be fancy doe - there's multiple levels
-You have to jump into or onto enemies to kill them - just walking into them will gurt you!
-Colored enemies have extra health or more power - Beware!

-Press "B" to begin!
